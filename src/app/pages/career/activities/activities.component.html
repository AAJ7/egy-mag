EGYMAG activities

We concentrate on improving BSF units for rearing and reproduction in order to obtain more larvae in two ways:

Increase capacity and collaboration in insect production and processing

Investigating, optimising, and scaling up BSF rearing technology

And so,

1- achieving BSF farming, consuming food waste, and providing BSF meal as a feed ingredient for pigs, chickens, and fish. In order to close the gap
and meet the rising demand for protein sources, more fish, meat, and eggs were produced.

2- The second by-product (frass) of BSF rearing is used as organic fertiliser because it is enriched with chitin and other beneficial elements for the soil,
resulting in higher crop yields (>70%) and higher income.

EGYMAG outcomes

EGYMAG is attempting to deliver outcomes that are in accordance with the (sustainable animal feed, sustainable increase in fish, pig and poultry production,
enhancing women and youth employment. increasing income from sale of BSF as feed component by creation of a supply chain that did not exist previously).
