Innovafeed is currently in the midst of an acceleration phase having demonstrated the scalability of its model with the opening of its Nesle site in November 2020,
just 18 months after the foundation stone was laid.

The largest active insect production site in the world

This production unit opened in Nesle in November 2020 is co-located
with Tereos (starch manufacturer) and Kogeban (biomass plant),
a unique example of industrial collaboration.

It will enable Innovafeed to acquire a supply of quality raw materials
and, for Tereos, to locally valorize its co-products while limiting
the energy needs associated with their processing.
